## Each shot — what it does

- Brand background (`BrandBg` with accent glow)

### 2. PlatformReveal (cutaway, 9.20–12.20s, 3.0s @ 30fps = 90 frames)
- Browser frame with URL `konten.com` typed into the address bar over 30 frames
- Below: tagline "Platform untuk Klipper cari cuan" rise-in at f40
- 3 feature pills appear sequentially @ f55 / f65 / f75: "Auto-connect akun" / "Tanpa form ribet" / "Langsung cair"

### 3. PayoutBreakdown (cutaway, 31.20–34.60s, 3.4s @ 30fps = 102 frames)

- Animated calculator on the right: "1.000 views × Rp 5 = Rp 5.000" with the "5.000" highlighted at f75
- Subtle pulse on the number at f80 to emphasize the payoff

### 4. OutroCard (cutaway, 44.70–47.00s, 2.3s @ 30fps = 69 frames)
- Brand background
- Wordmark reveal (centered, big): `konten.com` with the dot in accent color

- Small CTA: "konten.com" pill at the bottom

## What stays as talking head
Everything between/around these spans: the build-up to the hook (0–0.3s), the explanation paragraphs (3.6–9.2s, 12.2–31.2s, 34.6–44.7s) — those keep the original head + audio untouched. That's the bulk of the trust-building.

## What I'm NOT doing in v1
- No overlays (small persistent CTAs) — adds complexity, can layer in later
- No intro title card before the hook — the "uang 500 juta" punch IS the hook
- No music/SFX pass — that's step 4, separate session
- No voice cleanup — that's step 3, depends on the noise floor of the actual recording (skip until needed)
- No thumbnails/packaging — that's step 5
